The Official Agreements

By entering this sacred space, you acknowledge and agree to the following binding contracts.

CONFIDENTIALITY & NON-CUDDLING AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into on this 1st day of July, 2025, by and between:

Cameron Higginbotham, hereinafter referred to as the "Enforcer",

and

All others, hereinafter referred to as the "Cuddlee".

Collectively, the "Parties".

  1. Purpose
    The purpose of this Agreement is to establish and confirm the understanding that couch cuddling is strictly prohibited under the terms outlined herein.
  2. Terms of Non-Cuddling
    The Cuddlee agrees to the following:
    • No cuddling, spooning, nuzzling, leaning, or otherwise engaging in cozy physical proximity on the couch or sofa shall occur.
    • Casual proximity (e.g., sitting side-by-side) is permitted only with a minimum of one (1) decorative throw pillow acting as a buffer.
    • The Enforcer reserves the right to enforce a "no contact zone" of up to 24 inches if deemed necessary.
  3. Confidentiality
    The Cuddlee agrees to maintain full confidentiality regarding:
    • Any temptation, disappointment, or longing related to couch cuddling.
    • Internal thoughts or spoken expressions such as "but it's cold" or "just for five minutes".
    • Any previous cuddling incidents on said couch, which are now considered null and void.
  4. Exceptions
    • Exceptions to this Agreement may only be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
    • Exception examples include but are not limited to: emotional emergencies, horror movie nights, or birthdays (with prior approval).
  5. Enforcement
    Violation of this Agreement may result in:
    • Verbal warnings (stern but loving).
    • Relocation to a separate piece of furniture.
    • One (1) week of remote-controlled privileges being revoked.
  6. Term
    This Agreement shall remain in effect until:
    • One or both Parties mutually agree to revoke it in writing, or
    • The Enforcer feels like cuddling again (this is non-negotiable and entirely at the Enforcer's discretion).

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on this 1st day of July, 2025, by and between:

Cameron, hereinafter referred to as the "Enforcer",

and

Others, hereinafter referred to as the "Kissee".

Collectively, the "Parties".

  1. Purpose
    The purpose of this Agreement is to establish and confirm the understanding that kissing of any kind is strictly prohibited under the terms outlined herein.
  2. Terms of Non-Kissing
    The Kissee agrees to the following:
    • No kissing, pecking, smooching, cheek brushing, lip grazing, or any other form of affectionate mouth-based contact shall occur.
    • Accidental proximity (e.g., brushing faces while reaching for snacks) will be met with immediate spatial readjustment.
    • The Enforcer reserves the right to establish a "No Lips Zone" with a minimum radius of six (6) inches at any time.
  3. Confidentiality
    The Kissee agrees to maintain full confidentiality regarding:
    • Any urges, cravings, or momentary lapses in judgment relating to kissing.
    • Utterances such as "just a little one" or "but we already kissed before."
    • Any past kisses, near-kisses, or hypothetical future kisses, which are now considered null, void, and inadmissible in contract negotiations.
  4. Exceptions
    • Exceptions to this Agreement may only be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
    • Exception examples include but are not limited to: national holidays, moments of shared victory, or spontaneous declarations of love (pending Enforcer approval).
  5. Enforcement
    Violation of this Agreement may result in:
    • Verbal reminders ("We signed a contract.")
    • Denial of flirt privileges
    • One (1) week of eye contact suspension (romantic gaze ban enforced)
  6. Term
    This Agreement shall remain in effect until:
    • One or both Parties mutually agree to revoke it in writing, or
    • The Enforcer initiates a kiss (this supersedes all prior terms and is final, with zero obligation to explain).
